# Screenshot checklist

Capture real-report screenshots only when the report licence and assignment instructions permit it, and keep them outside Git.

- Show the `/find-rpt` command and the complete brief status.
- Show one inline citation being opened in the loopback viewer.
- Confirm the correct report and page without exposing an absolute local path.
- Confirm the highlight is narrow and supports the rendered claim.
- Include a not-found or ambiguity stop result.
- Include a synthetic escalation draft ending with the explicit not-sent statement.
- Crop or redact report passages, values, watermarks, names, email addresses, phone numbers, filenames, and citation IDs unless disclosure is explicitly permitted.
- Check the image metadata and surrounding desktop for usernames, paths, notifications, or secrets.
- Store the final image outside the repository and perform a second-person or second-pass review before submission.
